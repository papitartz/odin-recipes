Classic Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is one of the oldest known cocktails. A combination of whiskey, bitters, and simple syrup. Drink this cocktail anytime of the year, and enjoy the sweetness

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger bourbon whiskey
  • 1 slice orange
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Pour simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass; stir to combine. Add ice cubes and pour in bourbon. Garnish with orange slice and maraschino cherry
