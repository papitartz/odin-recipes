Moscow Mule

This is the recipe for the famous drink, the "Moscow Mule". It is a great drink to have on a night out on the town. It's a perfect drink if you're looking for a sour and sweet drink, served in a stylish copper mug

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
  • Ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
  2. Drop lime wedge into the mug for garnish
